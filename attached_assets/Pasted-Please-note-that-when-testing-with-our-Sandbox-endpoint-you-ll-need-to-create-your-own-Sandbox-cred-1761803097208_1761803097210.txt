1. Subscriptions

- The initial transaction undergoes the 3D secure authentication process.
- The payload sent to our endpoint specifies the cycles, frequency, and recurring amount.
- Upon the transaction's success, the token is received via your notify URL/callback URL.
- Our server automatically processes subscriptions based on the initial payload, including cycles, frequency, recurring amount, and the specified run date.
- You can update the subscription to adjust the run date, amount, or frequency.
- Subscriptions can be canceled either through your Payfast dashboard or via API.

2. Tokenization

- The initial transaction also undergoes 3D secure authentication.
- Upon transaction success, your notify URL/callback URL will receive the token.
- You can use the token on demand, specifying the amount and date of your choice.
- We expect a payload from your server to charge the token, as this process is not automated from our end.
